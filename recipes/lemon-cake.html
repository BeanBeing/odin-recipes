Delicious Homemade Lemon Cake

This is a Lemon Cake who's purpose is to provide a nice sweet lemonly flavor onto your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps/Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  2. Beat sugar and butter together in a mixing bowl using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  3. Sift flour and baking powder together in a separate bowl; add to creamed mixture. Pour in milk, lemon zest, and lemon juice and mix until you achieve a smooth batter. Spoon batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 35 minutes.
